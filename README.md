# 🚢 Cruise Crews

**Stay Connected at Sea**

Cruise Crews is a Progressive Web App (PWA) for cruise passengers to communicate via group chats, private messages, walkie-talkie push-to-talk, video calls, and image sharing — all while on a cruise.

## Features

- **Group Chats** — Create and join crews by cruise ship, with public, private, and invite-only modes
- **Direct Messages** — Private one-on-one messaging with shipmates
- **Walkie-Talkie** — Push-to-talk voice broadcasting to your crew
- **Video Chat** — In-app video calling with camera controls
- **Image & GIF Sharing** — Share photos and GIFs in any chat
- **Cruise Info** — Enter your voyage details and share with your crew
- **QR Code Invites** — Generate QR codes and shareable links to invite people to your crew
- **PWA** — Installable on iOS and Android, works offline

## Tech Stack

- Pure HTML/CSS/JavaScript (single-file PWA)
- Hosted on Cloudflare Pages
- Service Worker for offline support and push notifications
- Custom domain: [cruise-crews.com](https://cruise-crews.com)

## Deployment

This repository is connected to **Cloudflare Pages** for automatic deployments.

Every push to the `main` branch automatically deploys to:
- https://cruise-crews.com
- https://cruise-crews.pages.dev
